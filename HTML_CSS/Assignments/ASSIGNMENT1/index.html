My unexpected trip through small gastronomic gems in Toronto

Posted: October 3, 2023 | Arnulfo Sánchez

I had been waiting for a long time for the right time to pack my suitcase and travel for a few days through Canada. Due to COVID-19, it took me a little time to go, I decided to tell some friends so that they could live this extraordinary experience with me in which we would not only visit cultural places and amazing landscapes but also, we would also have time to try the gastronomy of this country, which is characterized by having different flavors because it has a wide variety of restaurants. That's why without having any doubt I grabbed my backpack, and passport and took a flight which I was sure I would never forget.

Everything started with a meeting with my friends months before traveling, we were very eager to visit some of the best places in Toronto. Even though we were deciding our plans and places to visit, food was the only important topic I wanted to talk about. Since Toronto is the most multicultural city in the world, it presents a wide variety of gastronomic delights, in this way you can go to different types of restaurants in just one city. While my friends decided on the itinerary, I only dedicated myself to searching for the restaurants I wanted to visit.

Because it was difficult to choose which restaurants to visit, I made a filter in which price and quality, recommendation, and rating were fundamental. I decided to make this blog in which I will recommend some of the best restaurants in my opinion.

My Top 5:

  1. Cactus Club

    1. Cactus Club was one of the first places we went, I had heard that it is an amazing place where our stomachs would be delighted by its amazing flavor. Without hesitation, we went for our first day's meal where I ordered salmon accompanied by vegetables and mashed potatoes, and a dessert which was very delicious. In general, the place is very interesting, its service and food are phenomenal, and because there were many people it makes its atmosphere particular since the music sets it up a lot.

  2. Earls

    1. The restaurant Earls brings me back many memories of a magnificent night that I will never forget, for the first time I ate poke, it is a bowl with different toppings. I loved the mix of vegetables, mango rice, and salmon. In addition to the poke, we had a pizza and French fries. To be honest I don't know which dish was the best!!!!, but I would definitely go again and I'm sure it would be the order, although what I miss the most is the fresh flavor of the salmon in the poke.

  3. Sinjeon Topokki

    1. I have some Korean friends who told me that Sinjeon Topokki is one of the best restaurants to eat topokki, I did a little search and it is a great restaurant chain in South Korea They only have one headquarters in Toronto and even though their establishment is small, their topokki is very delicious and the price is very affordable.

  4. Rio 40 restaurant

    1. Rio 40 is a Brazilian restaurant with a very good atmosphere and music, in this I ordered one of their most typical dishes which is STROGONOFF DE FRANGO OU CARNE, its flavor was wonderful, I liked the mix of flavors and because its Latin flavor reminds me of the food at home. I had a very pleasant time and in the evening it is a place that is used more for socializing.

  5. Insomnia restaurant

    1. In Insomnia, I have tried one of the best burger I have ever eaten in my life! The burger is called Insomnia Burger, it had a phenomenal flavor that melted in your mouth, a very well-balanced mix of flavors and it was very juicy. This place is very popular for brunch as well as drinks since the place has a bar to have some cocktails.
